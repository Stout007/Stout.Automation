TogeBot

Automated Discord Betting Management

What is TogeBot?

TogeBot revolutionizes in-game betting management for Discord servers. Previously, admins spent countless hours manually collecting bet information through tickets, typing data, and managing spreadsheets. TogeBot automates this entire workflow with an intuitive button-based system that captures all betting data and seamlessly transfers it to Google Sheets.

Example Workflow

  • User clicks "Place Bet" button in Discord
  • Interactive form collects all necessary information
  • Data is automatically validated and formatted
  • Information instantly populates your Google Sheet
  • Admins receive confirmation and can manage bets effortlessly
